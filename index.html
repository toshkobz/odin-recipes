Pizza

Pesto pizza is a great alternative to your regular pizza!

In order to prepare the pizza you would need a few ingredients which we will list below including the instructions.

Have fun and make sure you save us a slice to taste the pizza!

Ingredients

  • Pesto pizza is a great alternative to your regular pizza.
  • ½ cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
